Ways To Reduce Carbon Footprint!


•Start eating food products that are in season.

•Do not buy farmed fish only buy wild caught fish.

•When traveling around town you can walk, run, cycle, or take public transportation instead of taking a personal car

•When going to work or school you can consider carpooling with somebody.

•In your houses just turning the A/C or heat down 1 degree can help make a small difference.

•Taking cooler showers will help not use up as much heat and in turn will help with your impact.
